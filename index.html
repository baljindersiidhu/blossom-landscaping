Our Story

Born in Calgary. Built for Calgary.

Blossom Landscaping & Maintenance Ltd. was founded with a simple mission: to bring world-class outdoor living to Calgary homeowners. What started as a small lawn care operation has grown into Calgary's premier full-service landscaping and outdoor construction company.

We understand Calgary's unique climate — from the vibrant summers to the harsh winters. Our team is trained, licensed, and passionate about creating outdoor spaces that work beautifully year-round.

From stunning stone patios in McKenzie Towne to professional snow removal in Tuscany, we've built a reputation for quality, reliability, and exceptional customer care across every Calgary neighbourhood.
